Family means unity to me. Our house is strictly copying this motto. From the outside it is tough and strong to protect and assure the safety. Inside it is warm, cosy and unifying, respecting the needs of its inhabitants. Accordingly, there is an atelier, office, game or even guest room downstairs. Upstairs there is a big kitchen for Mum, a big garage for Dad and two terraces for all of us to spend time together.

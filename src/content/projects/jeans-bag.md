A bag born from what others throw away. Old jeans were taken apart and reassembled — pocket by pocket — into something new and entirely useful. The pockets from different pairs create a landscape of compartments, each with its own character and size. The question is part of the experience: which pocket did I put it in? Made years ago and still in use.

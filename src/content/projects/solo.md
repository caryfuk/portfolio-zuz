Water level, a symbol of infinity, has been attracting the eye of a mankind for centuries. Apartment houses with their water edge composition offer a mixture of holiday like feeling and everyday life in the capital city. Hotel resort and retail outlets built on the water create a contrast to nearby busy city rush, enabling its visitors a total relief from working stress. What a delightful mixture, mixed urban zone.

Water level, a symbol of infinity, has been attracting the eye of a mankind for centuries. Apartment houses with their water edge composition offer a mixture of holiday like feeling and everyday life in capital city. Elementary house forms are scattered in a circle around and across the lake Rohlik.
The hotel rooms floating on the surface is something the city hasn't seen before. A new neighbourhood that belongs to the water.

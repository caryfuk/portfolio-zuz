A lamp shade made from packaging airbags — the kind that usually end up in the bin after a delivery. Rolled, shaped and fixed with simple adhesive tape, they wrap around an ordinary ceiling bulb and transform it completely. The translucent plastic diffuses the light softly, creating a cloud-like glow. No tools, no skills required. Just looking at what you already have differently.
